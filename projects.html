Bonsai

MOAB

MOAB is a simple ball balancing machine which balances a ball on unstable plate.

MOAB is built using BONSAI which is AI based technology used to create brains.

After training of MOAB brain when exported, the trained AI will keep the ball in the center of the plate for as long as possible.

The Moab sample uses a simulation for brain training. A single ball balances on a movable plate. The simulation model tracks the position of the ball on an (x,y) plane, the velocity of the ball, and the changing angle of the plate over time. On every training iteration, Bonsai receives an ObservableState object from the simulator with the following state information:



MOAB brain is created with help of INKLING language which is AI based language specifically designed for training brains in Bonsai by microsoft.

Concept list

States

MoveToCenter takes ObservableState as input, which means it can access all of the available simulation state information during training.

  • ball_x:
  • ball_y
  • ball_vel_x
  • ball_vel_y

Sim Actions

On each training iteration, the brain can alter the angle of the plate to alter the velocity of the ball:

  • input_pitch
  • input_roll

Goal Objectives

  • Objective: Do not allow the ball to fall off the plate
  • Type: avoid
  • Tracking value: current ball position (ball_x, ball_y)
  • Range to avoid: the outer edge of the plate as defined by 20% of the radius.

OBSTACLE AVOID ROBO

This robo avoids the obstacle by sensing the distance with help of ultrasonic sensor. When obstacle is in front of the robo and if the distance between obstacle and robo is less then 10 cm then it move one step back rotates the ultrasonic left and right with help of servo motor. If left distance is greater then right side distance then the robo will turn left (90 degree turn) and vice versa.
